2. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services to you. This includes:

Data Provided by You:

  • **Identity and Contact Data:** Name, email address, phone number, and account passwords.
  • **Transaction Data:** Details about products/services purchased, payment method (though we do not store full credit card details), and billing address.
  • **Local Preference Data:** Reviews, ratings, saved favorites, and marketing subscription preferences.

Data Collected Automatically (Local Focus):

  • **Location Data:** We collect **approximate geographic location** (e.g., city or ZIP code) based on your IP address to deliver localized content, show nearby businesses, and check delivery zones. **We do not track precise, real-time GPS location unless explicitly permitted by your device settings for a specific service (e.g., live driver tracking).**
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our platform, including search queries for local businesses, pages viewed, and links clicked.
  • **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and time zone settings.

3. How We Use Data

We use your data for the following local marketing and operational purposes:

  • To **Fulfill Orders and Services** (e.g., verifying local delivery addresses).
  • To **Personalize Your Experience** by showing you local deals, relevant businesses, and nearby community events.
  • For **Local Marketing and Promotions** of relevant community businesses, provided you have opted in.
  • To **Improve our Platform** by analyzing localized usage patterns and service area performance.
  • To **Maintain Security** and detect fraudulent activity specific to local transactions.

4. Sharing Your Data (Local Focus)

We only share your data with trusted third parties necessary for running our marketplace:

  • **Local Business Partners:** We share necessary information (e.g., delivery address, order items) with the **local business** you order from to fulfill your transaction.
  • **Payment Processors:** Shared securely with payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to process payments.
  • **Service Providers:** Shared with vendors who help us run our service (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, email distribution).
  • **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose data when legally required (e.g., court order).
**Crucial Note on Location:** We **do not sell** precise location data to third-party marketers. Your location data is used *only* to facilitate relevant local services and internal analytics.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information.

  • **Preference Cookies:** Used to remember your local settings and preferences (e.g., saved ZIP code, language).
  • **Analytics Cookies:** Used to analyze how users interact with our local marketing content.
